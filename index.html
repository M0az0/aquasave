About SDG 6

Clean Water is
Everyone's Right

SDG 6 aims to ensure the availability and sustainable management of clean water and sanitation for all people by 2030.

In parts of rural Sub-Saharan Africa and Egypt's own western desert communities, women and children walk an average of 6 kilometres daily to collect water — water that is often contaminated. Every 2 minutes, a child under 5 dies from a water-related disease. AquaSave exists because this is preventable.

Our platform helps individuals understand and reduce their daily water waste through awareness, interactive tracking, and smart tips. When millions of people save a little — the impact is enormous.

Turn Off the Tap

Turn off tap while brushing teeth or washing hands. Saves up to 12 liters per minute.

Saves ~24L/day

Fix Leaks Immediately

A dripping faucet wastes over 3,000 liters per year. Fix leaks as soon as they appear.

Saves ~8L/day

Bucket Over Hose

Use a bucket instead of a hose for cars or plants — uses 80% less water.

Saves ~150L/wash

Shorter Showers

Cutting your shower from 10 to 5 minutes saves about 50 liters every time.

Saves ~50L/shower

Water Plants at Night

Watering in early morning or evening reduces evaporation by up to 70%.

Saves ~30L/day

Reuse Cooking Water

Cool pasta or vegetable water can be used to water plants — waste nothing.

Saves ~5L/meal
