Smart Tips

Daily Water
Conservation Tips

Small habits, massive impact. Start with just one today.

Turn Off the Tap

Turn off tap while brushing teeth or washing hands. Saves up to 12 liters per minute.

Saves ~24L/day

Fix Leaks Immediately

A dripping faucet wastes over 3,000 liters per year. Fix leaks as soon as they appear.

Saves ~8L/day

Bucket Over Hose

Use a bucket instead of a hose for washing cars or watering plants — uses 80% less water.

Saves ~150L/wash

Shorter Showers

Cutting your shower from 10 to 5 minutes saves about 50 liters every time.

Saves ~50L/shower

Water Plants at Night

Watering in early morning or evening reduces evaporation by up to 70%.

Saves ~30L/day

Reuse Cooking Water

Cool pasta or vegetable water can be used to water plants — waste nothing.

Saves ~5L/meal
